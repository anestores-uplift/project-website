Resident ID System

About The System

Green Appleâ€™s Resident Information System helps Local Government Units (LGUs) manage and maintain relevant information of their constituents. The system consists of a physical ID card given to each resident, as well as a database system that maintains their relevant information. The ID Card is tamper-proof and RFID-based which has a unique serial number assigned to each resident. The database system can be stored in your local server or hosted in a secured cloud platform which can be queried for reports by your authorized personnel.

Components

  • Resident ID Card
    • Radio Frequency based Identification (RFID) Card
    • Logo of the LGU
    • Photo and Signature of the Resident
    • Basic Personal Information (Full Name, Address, Date of Birth)
  • Resident Information Management System
    • Data Gathering App
    • Resident Masterfile
    • Customized Queries and Reports

Benefits & Uses

  • For the LGU
    • Easy identification of residents
    • Access to relevant demographic information for planning
    • Means for efficient roll-out of programs and services
    • For Businesses/Employers to easily hire residents of city/municipality
  • For the Resident
    • Proof of identification
    • Can serve as secondary ID accepted in other government and financial institutions
    • Priority access to the LGU's programs and services
