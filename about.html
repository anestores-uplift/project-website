Who We Are

Established in 2015, Green Apple Technologies & Systems, Incorporated is an information technology company committed to deliver innovative products that will empower and elevate Local Government Units (LGUs) in the Philippines as well as companies in the private sector. Its re-engineering solutions enables clients to have optimum control, higher efficiency and real-time data to make informed decisions.

Our Mission

To ensure integrity and innovation in everything that we do. To promote smart leadership and governance through the use of modern technologies and adequate opportunities for growth that will benefit our stakeholders, customers, employees and the community as a whole.

Our Vision

To be an indispensable partner of Local Government Units and Private Companies in their quest towards growth and development, through our commitment of providing highly reliable I.T. solutions, top-level customer service and competent advisory.
