Developed by Green Apple Technologies & System Inc., REACH presents a unique platform to identify and reach the target voting population by segregating the voters through different affiliations.

Designed for the holistic campaign and election experience, it is a tool that would last for the entire duration of your candidacy. Through gathering all the details about the voters for a specific area, and after cross-referencing across lists supplied by the government, search and tagging tools are mobilized.
