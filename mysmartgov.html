MySMARTGov is your LGU’s bridge to eReadiness success. Complete with the latest technology and a competent implementation and support team, the system modernizes the processing of government transactions, providing for a streamlined, efficient, and transparent delivery of public service.

System Modules

  • SmartSERVE
  • SmartFINANCE
  • SmartADMIN
  • SmartLEAD

SmartSERVE

Efficient delivery of public service and faster processing of revenue transactions is at the core of this module. This client-centered solution handles Business Permits and Licensing, Real Property Tax Assessment & Billing, Other Income Recording and Treasury transactions.

SmartFINANCE

This module enables the LGU to attain good financial housekeeping. Financial Accounting, Budget, Procurement, Inventory and Fixed Assets are the components of this module. It features a COA-compliant accounting system, paperless budget monitoring and a streamlined procurement process with online approval for the LCE and department heads.

SmartADMIN

Effectively maintains records of the LGU’s human capital, it centers on Human Resource Information System integrated with Time and Attendance and Payroll Systems.

SmartLEAD

An essential tool for the Local Chief Executive as well as Department Heads, this module is a set of real-time dashboards that give a bird’s eye view of all the relevant data from the different modules’ transactions. It supports and promotes informed decision-making.
